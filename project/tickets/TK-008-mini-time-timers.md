# Mini time timers (circular remaining-time indicators)

## Context

Visual "time timers" (circular pie-style indicators, as used in ADHD-friendly
physical timers) make remaining time graspable without reading numbers — a good fit
for the app's time-blindness mission ([SPEC-001]). Could apply per task
([SPEC-010]) and/or for the whole session ([SPEC-006]). Migrated from
`conductor/backlog.md` (TK-001).

## Notes

Idea stage — needs design discussion (where do they appear, do they replace or
complement the linear progress bars) and acceptance criteria before work starts.
